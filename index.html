About Me

I am Mahbuba Tasmin, a Bangladeshi female student in my final year at North South University,Dhaka,Bangladesh. My undergraduate major is in Computer Science and Engineering and I am also pursuing a minor degree in mathematics. I'm at present employed as a teaching assistant in the Department of Electrical and Computer Engineering at North South University. Besides, I am also highly affiliated with co-curricular activities and currently, I am leading the NSU(North South University) ACM Student Chapter as the Vice Chair of the community.

Beside my academic works, I am working under HCI and Human Centered Machine Learning research works under Dr. Nova Ahmed,Associate Professor and Ms. Tamanna Motahar,Lecturer at the Department of Electrical and Computer Engineering, North South University. From 2018 till the date, I have participated in ACM SIGCHI Summer School in India on Research Methodology of HCI, published a student research paper in UbiComp 2018,organized and participated in ACM SIGCHI Summer School in Bangladesh very recently in March 2019. I have worked on different kinds of research works those ranged from social computing, machine learning, and HCI and I did field work like data collection, taking interviews, data analysis, and prepared reports.

Before North South University, I have completed my high school from Feni Girls' Cadet College with distinction national scholarship.I'm voluntarily working for an online data analysis emerging company The R-squared Project

Besides coding and work, I love writing, travelling and photography. I'm also affiliated Bangladesh Youth Leadership Center, as a proud graduate of this community.
